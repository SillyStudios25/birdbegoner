Our Mission?: To get rid of these flying rats

Why you should choose us:

Our mission is to help prevent birds of any sort and any type from reaching into your bins and littering the ground around. Since 1997, birds have been terrorising us and the innocent children in Cecil Hills, by littering all over the place, framing the poor, innocent children. Our goal is to prevent that from happening.
